BIO

Arvind Jagassar graduated in 2020 with a BEd. in Geography Education, as well as an MSc. in Integrated Coast and Ocean Management, in 2022, from the University of Trinidad and Tobago. He gained interest in GIS while studying and working under his GIS lecturer, for both degrees. He also worked under the same lecturer as a GIS/Remote Sensing Research Assistant. He grew to love GIS after witnessing its applicative diversity. With his current GIS skills and the new skills he will acquire in the BTech GIS program, Arvind's focus is on Cartography as well as data analytics and visualization.
